Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a Suzuki teacher?

Contact us via email at teachers@pittsburghsuzuki.org with information about your child's age, instrument interest, and location. We'll provide you with contact information for member teachers in your area.

What age should my child start?

Suzuki students can begin as young as 3-4 years old, depending on the instrument and the child's readiness. Piano and violin are common starting instruments. Contact a teacher to discuss your child's readiness.

Do parents need to have musical experience?

No! Parent involvement is crucial in Suzuki education, but musical experience is not required. Parents learn alongside their children and provide support at home.

How do I become a member teacher?

Teachers interested in joining the Pittsburgh Suzuki Association should have completed Suzuki teacher training and be committed to the Suzuki philosophy. Contact us at info@pittsburghsuzuki.org for membership information.
