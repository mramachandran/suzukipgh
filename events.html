Monthly Group Classes

When: Various dates throughout the year

Where: Locations vary by instrument and level

Students are organized into group classes by instrument and approximate level. Contact your teacher for specific group class information and schedules.

Winter Workshop

Typical Time: January or February

An intensive day of learning featuring guest clinicians, master classes, and group sessions. Open to all students. Details and registration information are typically announced in the fall.

Spring Concert

Typical Time: April or May

Our annual celebration featuring performances from students of all levels. This event showcases the progress and achievements of Pittsburgh Suzuki students and brings together our entire community.

Past Events

The Pittsburgh Suzuki Association has a rich history of bringing together students, teachers, and families through music. Our past events have included:

  • Annual spring concerts and recitals
  • Technique workshops with renowned Suzuki teachers
  • Chamber music opportunities and collaborations
  • Play-ins and community performances
  • Teacher training and professional development sessions
